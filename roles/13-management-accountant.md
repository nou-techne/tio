# Role 13: Management Accountant

**Advisory Role** — Tracks internal economics, dashboards, scenario modeling for Financial Systems Committee.

## Mandate
Produce legible financial views; model patronage sensitivities; maintain chart of accounts.

## Artifacts Produced
- Live dashboard (revenue/labor/community weights visualization).
- Scenario reports (e.g., "If labor 50%, impact on top 20% members").
- Monthly management accounts reconciliation.

## Inputs Required
- Habitat event stream.
- Member contribution logs.

## Sprint Triggers
- Allocation formula changes.
- Committee reviews.

## Success Criteria
- 95% automation in reporting.
- Committee decisions backed by models.

*Advisory: Supports Economic Systems Engineer (11).*
